Our Transportation Services

A full range of non-emergency medical transport services tailored to ambulatory and wheelchair-accessible needs.

Ambulatory Transport

Door-to-door service for patients who can walk independently or with minimal assistance, in clean and comfortable vehicles.

Wheelchair Transport

Spacious, ADA-compliant vans equipped with hydraulic lifts and secure four-point restraint systems for safe wheelchair rides.

Dialysis & Recurring Visits

Dependable scheduled transport for ongoing dialysis, chemotherapy and rehabilitation — never miss an appointment.

Hospital Discharges

Comfortable, attentive rides home after a hospital stay — with assistance from the bedside to your front door.

Doctor Appointments

On-time pickup and return service for routine checkups and specialist medical visits across the region.

Senior & Assisted Living

Patient, friendly transport for seniors — including hands-on help to and from the vehicle and into appointments.

Driven by Care, Defined by Service

Our mission shapes every ride we provide — safety, punctuality and dignity for every passenger we serve.

01

Safety First

Every driver is background-checked, drug-tested, and trained in passenger assistance and defensive driving techniques.

02

On-Time, Every Time

We respect your schedule and your health. Punctual pickups and timely arrivals to every appointment are our promise.

03

Dignified Service

We treat every passenger with kindness, patience, and the respect they deserve — like family, every single trip.

04

Clean, Modern Fleet

Our vehicles are sanitized after every ride and maintained to the highest mechanical and comfort standards.

05

Family Communication

We keep loved ones informed every step of the way — from pickup confirmation to safe drop-off.

06

Accessible to All

From ambulatory to wheelchair-bound passengers, we tailor every ride to the unique needs of those we serve.

Our Promise

More Than Transportation — Peace of Mind

We understand that medical transport is more than just getting from point A to point B. It's about trusting someone with the well-being of you or your loved one during a vulnerable time.

About Us

Compassionate Care On Every Mile

Founded by Ms. Iesha Campbell, Aegis Medical Transport LLC was built on a simple belief: every patient deserves safe, on-time, and respectful transportation to and from their medical appointments.

From dialysis sessions to physical therapy, doctor visits, and hospital discharges — our trained drivers and clean, comfortable vehicles ensure peace of mind for patients and families alike.

Our Services

Non-Emergency Medical Transport You Can Trust

We provide a full range of NEMT services tailored to ambulatory and wheelchair-accessible needs.

Ambulatory Transport

Door-to-door service for patients who can walk independently or with minimal assistance.

Wheelchair Transport

Spacious, ADA-compliant vans equipped with lifts and secure restraints for safe rides.

Dialysis & Recurring Visits

Dependable scheduled transport for ongoing treatments — never miss an appointment.

Hospital Discharges

Comfortable rides home after a hospital stay, with care from arrival to drop-off.

Doctor Appointments

On-time pickup and return for routine and specialist medical visits.

Senior & Assisted Living

Patient, friendly transport for seniors — including help to and from the vehicle.
